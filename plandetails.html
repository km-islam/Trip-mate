Itinerary

The world’s largest contiguous mangrove forest hosts an amazing faunal & floral diversity, including the probably largest remaining population of Royal Bengal tigers, the endangered estuarine crocodile, Ganges River dolphins as well as an astounding variety of birds. Join our Adventure Cruise and explore this intricate maze of tidal waterways, a natural treasure of Bangladesh.

Join our Adventure Cruise and explore this intricate maze of tidal waterways, a natural treasure of Bangladesh.

Day 01: Khulna- Harbaria- Kochikhali (onboard)

The tour coordinator will meet at Jailkhana Ghat/Rupsha Ghat in Khulna and will have a short brief regarding the tour and formalities with boarding assistance. After completion of the boarding, the boat will begin the cruise to the south. After a few hours of cruising the boat will stop at a forest station to complete formalities and also pick up armed forest guards to stay onboard. Your Sundarban journey has been started through the world largest Mangrove forest. We’ll stop at Harbaria eco-center, a popular spot of Sundarbans for a walking. Continue to Kachikhali for overnight. Lunch & dinner on board. Overnight at the vessel.

Day 02: Kachikhali – Dimer Char - Kotka -Tambulbunia (on board)

This early morning the motorboat continuous to the bay of narrow canals, Dramatic excitement begins when you switch over to ‘country-boat’ rowing along with the picturesque view of thickness Mangrove forest in Kachikhali with absolute silence prevails-discover pugmarks of ‘Royal Bengal Tiger’ various species of Kingfisher and home of the crocodile. Later on, move forward to Dimer Char. Dimer Char is a tidal flat(s) located in Sundarban. Time to explore the Kotka wildlife of Sundarbans. Don does not miss out capturing spotted deer (Chital Horin), different species of macaque Monkeys and mongoose, wild boar, endanger reptiles and Indian python, and explore as many species of birds out 260. The boat will be anchored at Tambulbunia for the night. Lunch & dinner on board. Overnight at the vessel.
